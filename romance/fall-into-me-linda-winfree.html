Bar owner Angel Henderson isn't looking for a man. Past experiences have her ready to swear off men for life, or at least to swear off giving her heart to anyone, especially the younger man determined to prove her wrong and show her that he could be just right for her.

Sheriff's deputy Troy Lee Farr is getting tired of having to prove himself at work and to the woman he's falling for. He needs her to understand he's more than just a fun date, and they could be mean more to each other if she'd only give them a chance.

But even the tentative happiness they find is threatened when Angel's past and Troy Lee's present intrude on what's starting to grow between them.
