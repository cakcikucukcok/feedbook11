In her debut book, Only God Can Do It: The Story Behind The Song, Evangelist Renee Winston shares her personal testimony of betrayal, rejection, and hurt to encourage her readers to keep the faith during seasons of adversity.

Sometimes, while we’re in the midst of our storms, we lose sight of the main thing we’re called to do as believers, which is to cling to God. He alone is the author and the finisher of our faith.

Only God Can Do It: The Story Behind The Song explores what it means to foster close encounters with Jesus Christ, how to walk away from seasons of hurt and shame with grace, and how to stand firm when God pulls you away from situations and individuals that seek to destroy you!
