CAN PEOPLE SHARE THE SAME DREAM? I mean, seven or eight people, experiencing the same dream on the same night as they sleep in separate places - can that happen? Ross, a local church minister, finds his pastoral load becoming increasingly puzzling. At the same time, his wife, Cathy, becomes pre-occupied with the events surrounding a suspicious death. The next weeks will prove to be the busiest they've known, as they seek the truth yet discover the lives of their friends unravelling. Was the death, a murder? Was the accident, a deliberate act? Why all the secrets? And what was the significance of the dream? The Unicorn is Simon Rennie's first novel, based in a world known to him for over a quarter of a century. The author combines life experiences with fiction to create a story of gritty reality and suspenseful drama.
