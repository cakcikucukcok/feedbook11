This book brings us back the wisdom of the past, twelve unpublished fables, one for each month, with pretty illustrations and traditional tunes for tin whistle that the child will love immediately and the adult will appreciate for their delicacy. Have you ever wondered what a snail and a slug say to each other? Or what a horse does to a mischievous dog? And what a blue butterfly thinks? The animals talk to each other and think many wonderful things, you just have to listen to them.
