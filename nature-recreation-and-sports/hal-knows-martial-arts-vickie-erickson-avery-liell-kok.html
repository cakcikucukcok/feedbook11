Hal Johnson is an eleven year boy who is always happy go lucky and he loves going to martial arts classes but he doesn't tell everyone at school hes in martial arts . After Hal promotes to a new belt he still wishes that his best friend Tony Erickson could join Martial Arts with him. Tony has been asking his parents if he can join martial arts but his parents think if Tony joins the martial arts academy he will want to be fighting or worse yet he will get hurt. Tony is a very smart kid. But as Hal discovers that his best friend Tony is having trouble with Brady, he knows he has to stick up for his best friend but how will he do that? Brady thinks he runs the whole school. Most kids at school run from Brady if they see him coming. Can Hal with the help of two more friends help out his friend Tony without getting them all in trouble at school?
