Are you looking for answers as you struggle with grief, hurts, difficult circumstances, or suffering? "Living Through Grief With Spirituality, with Pictured Words of Comfort from Jeremiah the Prophet" relates our own struggles with grief and suffering to the hardships experienced by Jeremiah, an Old Testament prophet. We are challenged to develop a deeper spirituality through trusting God and accepting the comfort that He offers, no matter how much we are grieving. The book briefly explains Jeremiah's tragic experiences that led him to be called "the weeping prophet." Yet God used his horrible circumstances to bring about a depth of spirituality that resulted in his sharing many words of comfort and encouragement that we can benefit from. These wonderful verses of encouragement and comfort are displayed on beautiful nature pictures. You can pick a verse, enjoy its picture, and sit and meditate on the promise it gives you. You'll find your own spiritual growth just as he did!
