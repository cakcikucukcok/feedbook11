In a world filled with cell phones, iPads, and all the latest interactive gadgets, Wisdom of Innocence challenges parents to put aside the devices and interact with their children. The text is an insightful yet practical guide detailing ways to improve parenting young children through listening, engaging, and paying attention to our young people. The inspiration for the authors drive to share this invaluable information with other parents comes from his daughter, Abigail. He hopes to inspire parents to really listen and engage with our youth, as we are raising our future. He interestingly observes that when parents start listening to their children, they may be surprised of the wisdom our innocent youth have to offer. In fact, he reminds us that we are all life learners and sometimes our greatest teachers come in smaller forms. Overall, Wisdom of Innocence is a thought-provoking guide that every parent will benefit from through an expanded and enhanced consciousness.
