Have you ever wondered what happens to your teeth after the Tooth Fairy takes them? Well it is time for your questions to be answered. Join Nick on this captivating mystery as he solves this age-old riddle. In the Tooth Fairy Mystery, put on your detective hats and get ready to uncover the Tooth Fairy's secrets. Children and parents alike should enjoy in these fully illustrated pages the whimsical story of a young boy's curiosity and the enchanting secret world he discovers as he hitches a ride along with the Tooth Fairy. Illustrated by her cousin Mia Thompson and inspired by her brother Nick, author Samantha D'Alonzo invites you to join her and her brother on The Tooth Fairy Mystery-her debut short story, written when she was only five years old.
