We all have challenging people situations that we need to deal with off and on. And its good to be prepared for these situations when they arise.

Sometimes we are at a loss as to how to handle some of them. This is where Lifelines comes to the rescue. Thats why I called the book Lifelines. It provides a lifeline to you in these situations.

Lifelines is a modern day book of practical wisdom, something we all need in order to be able to deal with and live comfortably with all types of people.

I suggest you read it regularly to get the full benefit. I am the author and I do. It serves as a reminder to me if I am falling short in any key area of my life and helps to put my dealings with people into perspective.. and this is something we all need.

If you wish to deal effectively and harmoniously with people situations and to broaden your mind, this book is for you.
