Public Defender Autry Holton is stuck both professionally and personally. If she refuses to defend the accused serial killer that tormented her town, she could be fired, not to mention disbarred. Then there's the fact the man she's been seeing has gotten cold feet, and that's before she managed to tell him she's pregnant...

Sheriff Stanton Reed knows he's not the right man for Autry. He already failed at one marriage, and he's sure he's just not husband material. So he breaks it off with Autry before things get any more complicated. But after an attempted break-in at Autry's home shows the real danger she's in, all he can think of is protecting her and keeping her close. And when it turns out she's pregnant with his baby, any hope of uncomplicated goes out the window.

Each book in the Hearts of the South series is a standalone story that can be enjoyed in any order.
