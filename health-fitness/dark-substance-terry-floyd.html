We are a group of individuals that are concerned with the direction that our world and the people in it are going. We are a group nameless individuals that offers a set of principles that allows all to live long, happy and prosperous lives free of negative programming. Every being uses these principles with every thought, emotion, word or action that they express. Some individuals uses these principles unknowingly in positive or negative ways; causing them to suffer needless mental, emotional and physical distress. We assist practitioners to identify and understand these principles so that they can use them knowingly in neutral/positive ways; for the betterment of themselves and mankind as a whole. Once the practitioners understand how to use these principles, their lives become harmonious and tremendously productive. They become creators of their lives and environments; which not only brings equality to them, but also to all of those beings around them.
