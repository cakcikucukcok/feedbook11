Imagination is the source that creativity spawn from, without it the soul would be void. This book basically reach those places in your mind that you try to reach in dreams and fantasies. I addressed the beauty of love, life, heartbreak, death and many other subjects. Each poem I collected over a long span of time. The unique poems talk to the readers on different levels. Some subjects are touchy and they will make us question ourselves. I titled the book "Ethereal Paradise" because the poems I selected causes the reader to extend beyond themselves, and grasp that higher elation that my poems have to offer. "Ethereal"--the term to me, I took it as 'airy.' While reading the poems they will make you feel as though you can fly. Get your copy and set sail upon a highly elevated paradise of ecstasy.
