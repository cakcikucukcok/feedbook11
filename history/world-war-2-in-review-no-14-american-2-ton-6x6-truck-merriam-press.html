Merriam Press World War 2 In Review Series. First eBook Edition 2017. This issue covers the American 2½-ton 6x6 medium truck series of World War II, the "deuce and a half," with coverage of the GMC CCKW 2½-ton 6x6 truck and the Studebaker US6 2½-ton 6x6 truck series. Five different designs were standardized by the U.S., two were also standardized by Canada. One was built primarily for Lend-Lease export during World War II. 289 photographs, drawings and illustrations.
