Rediscovering Americanism: And the Tyranny of Progressivism by Mark R. Levin | Conversation Starters

  In “Rediscovering Americanism: And the Tyranny of Progressivism”, readers are reintroduced to some of Mark Levin’s principles and ideas, all concerning the current state of America as a country and political giant. Each of the chapters in “Rediscovering Americanism” discusses something about the state of the United States’ government, revealing Levin’s fears about the future of the country and the impact modern politics will have on future generations.

  Levin’s novel is his fifth concerning American politics and conservatism. He is a prominent lawyer and radio personality, also contributing to televised and written media. Many readers enjoy “Rediscovering Americanism” for its upholding of Levin’s passion for upheaving the American political system and its power dynamics.
