Welcome to April's fantasy. 

This is a replica of the anonymous journal that captured April's heart in the feature film, April Flowers. 

A romantic quest across New York City, April Flowers tells the story of one woman's adventure as she seeks to return an anonymous journal left upon the subway. Captivated by the personal ramblings within, April builds a fantasy that complicates her real-life relationships. 

This is a collection of those writings. Comprised of poetry, personal confusion and a whole lot of feeling, the journal provides insight into the emotional connection April shares with the mysterious author, which could be any of us. 

A modern-day, coming-of-age tale, April Flowers stars Celina Jade, Jon Fletcher, Kate Middleton, and Keir Dullea.
