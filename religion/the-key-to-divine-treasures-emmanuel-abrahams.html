The treasures of God are endless; still, for every treasure of God, there is a key, and that key is the anointing. God loves to keep things plain and simple, all while hiding them in plain sight. The Key to Divine Treasures teaches believers how to gain unlimited access to Gods treasures and how to get anointed by God and his divine purpose. This study and guide recounts the lessons that Pastor Abrahams has learned over the years about the anointingthe process for receiving it and the realization that its possible to enjoy the treasures of God. The anointing is a secret of God that is only available to those who hold Him closely, in their hearts. As you study this fascinating subject, youll discover how to get all the things that God has reserved for each of his followers. The Key to Divine Treasures presents an opportunity to learn how to increase personal potential to attain greater blessings in the Kingdom of God.
