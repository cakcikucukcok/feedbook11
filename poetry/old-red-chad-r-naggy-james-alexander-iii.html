We all have value. No matter our age or size, we all have something special to add. Old Red has been serving the community he loves for many years. He puts out fires, responds to accidents, and helps the citizens of his community. When bigger, more modern fire trucks replace him, Old Red doesnt seem to be needed much any longer. See how Old Red and even another friend show that even though they are smaller and much older, they are still an important part of this community. You wouldnt think an rusty old fire engine could teach us all a lesson, but in this whimsically feel-good story, he does just that.
