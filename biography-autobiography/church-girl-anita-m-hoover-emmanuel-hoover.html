One foot in the world, the other in church...THE MIND GAME IS TOO MUCH! Raised in church from her childhood, Anita - like many others - gave in to the pull of peer-pressure. Included in that downward spiral was an abusive relationship that almost cost her life. Returning to the only comfort-zone she ever knew - the church, she still struggled to "belong". Through many life-altering events, the Lord got Anita's attention and let her know she had to make up her mind and make a change. Would she live for the Lord, or keep trying to live for herself? It is Anita's hope that this true story will sound the alarm to every reader: "IT'S TIME TO CHOOSE WHOM YOU WILL SERVE!"
