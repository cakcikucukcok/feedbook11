SPOOKY COOL IN A MANNER DELIGHTFULLY TRUE TO LIFE VAMPIRE IN THE HOUSE! A Vampire Romp For Readers Of All Ages What would living with a real vampire be like and how would the neighbors react? For that matter, how could this even come about? These issues and more are delightfully covered in a realistic manner that will leave you thinking and smiling too! Timely topics, biting insights, and people whose perspectives and views are enjoyable, insightful, and comfortably familiar, all come to life when the Wynn family discovers that their daughter, Julie, is more than just different. She is a vampire the real thing! From home and family, to doctors and politics, to the controversies and public reactions, this book offers an insider's view of what happened when the Wynn family had to deal with the realities of a Vampire In The House!
