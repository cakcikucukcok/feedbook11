Committing to selflessly praying for another person is the best gift you can offer. The Bible places an emphasis on the need for continual prayer, insisting that we “pray without ceasing” (1 Thessalonians 5:17). This principle is especially true for the women in your life, be it your wife, mother, daughter, sister, family member, employer, pastor, or friend.

 All the women God has strategically placed in your life need you to petition God on their behalf.

 This 21-day devotional is a guide to help you pray for these women with the complete assurance that there is no circumstance or hardship that they will ever face, that escapes the limits of God’s ability to accomplish His perfect will for their lives.

 The charge for you today is

if you love her, pray for her
