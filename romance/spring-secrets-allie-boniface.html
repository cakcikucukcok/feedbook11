Sienna Cruz is writing a dissertation about small towns and their secrets and scandals. Luckily, she has just the town to do her research in. She left Pine Point at age fifteen, after her mother's untimely death. She's come back to Pine Point now to take a job teaching special needs kids--at least that's what she's told everyone.

After eight years on the West Coast, Mike Springer has come home to Pine Point to open a local gym. He's ready to put his past behind him and have a fresh start in the small town he grew up in. He's not looking for a relationship, so being workout friends with benefits with the gorgeous Sienna seems like the perfect setup.

But Sienna's digging for skeletons, and when she finds one in her own closet, everyone's past and future is rocked to the core.

Each book in the Pine Point series is a standalone story that can be enjoyed in any order.
