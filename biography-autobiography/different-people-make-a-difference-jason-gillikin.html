Through prayer and God's grace this first time author wrote his life story. This is a story of a child who witnessed loss and heartache at such a young age. He found love from those in his life that he should, but was also rejected by those that should have loved him most. Get ready to get in touch with your emotions, to make a life-changing decision, and finally to realize that forgiveness is never easy; however, the consequences of not doing so can be something you carry with you forever.
