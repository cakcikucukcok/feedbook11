Feature Films: Broken Trust was filmed by Jane Fonda Films for TNT and starred Tom Selleck and Academy Award nominee Marsha Mason. The screenplay was written by Joan Didion and her husband John Gregory Dunne. The Bone Garden has been featured in multiple books and the Geraldo Rivera Show, and on The Discovery Channel. Most of Wood’s novels have been optioned for motion pictures and two were produced.
