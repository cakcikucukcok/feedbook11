It is dark before dawn. One definition of dawn is the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise. Another definition is the beginning of a phenomenon or period of time, especially one considered favorable. To the readers of this book you have already been introduced to the darker side of life by an author who not only lived, written, and interpreted the darker side of life. But one who fully understands that the darker side of life is right before dawn. I ask you to read and absorb the words he has written. For these words will illustrate vividly the transition from dark to light. Please be prepared to see the light in the sky before sunrise. Please be prepared for the beginning of a phenomenon especially favorable about to overtake and disperse the dark forces in your life and those around you.
