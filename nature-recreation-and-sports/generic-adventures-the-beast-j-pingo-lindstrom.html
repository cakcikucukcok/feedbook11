The Beast is a small, quick and easy adventure for any game group that wants a fast and simple adventure for a single night. The Beast is a generic adventure, created for ANY type of fantasy setting/world. The adventure is written without any rule system in mind. Most RPG rules based in this type of world should be possible to be used. In The Beast the adventurers enters a dungeon with the mission to find a treasure. But as they enter the dungeon, they find themselves trapped - and now they have to fight to survive! This is the second adventure in a series of cheap, quick and fun generic adventures by PenguinComics. These adventures also work perfectly fine to use with our rule system Drudge!
