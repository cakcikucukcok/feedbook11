“I want the children in my life to grow up to be strong, ethical leaders!” -Christina DeMara, Author

Early Life Leadership in Children: 101 Strategies to Grow Great Leaders is perfect if you have children, love and care for children, or work with children.

We all can look back at our lives and still remember the people who loved us and taught us valuable life lessons. Research says children look up to the adults in their lives, not celebrities. This book is a great tool to help you plant seeds of success in the children in your life.

Early Life Leadership in Children goes beyond the definition and examples of leadership. It offers you the platform and usable actions you need to cultivate leadership in the youngest generation. Christina’s research-based book will present each leadership skill, explain why it’s important, and give you a recommendation on how to teach it to the children you love or work with. You will have the ideas you need to nurture the future leaders!
