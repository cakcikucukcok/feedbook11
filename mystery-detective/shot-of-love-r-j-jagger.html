From the acclaimed pen of International Thriller Writer R.J. Jagger comes a pulse-pounding thriller that you won’t be able to put down.

Denver homicide detective Nick Teffinger has come across an edgy young woman who is passing through town. She’s wild and loose and mysterious but that doesn’t mean she isn’t perfect, with that surfer-girl hair and that surfer-girl body and up top, that face, that love-me-do or love-me-don’t face.  

In fact, it’s as if she’d sprung directly out of the secret corners of his brain where he stores images and songs and feelings and emotions and all the great little things that are worth getting into your life and holding them close, if you only can.

There is one negative, however. She’s either a killer or about to be killed, or both. Either way, she’s on the run, and now so is he.
