Prom Knights follows some of the adventures of a high school junior as he prepares for his junior prom. Everyday life in a small rural area in the Wild West is highlighted with the antics of Bug. This happy-go-lucky young man faces life with a smile and enjoys the loyalty of a close friend. Together they become partners in the rescue of a young lady who, because of a horrible accident, was on the verge of missing one of the most memorable nights of her life. With several hilarious twists in what would normally be a routine process of preparing for the prom, Bug and his friend end up involved in both heartwarming and emotional events that lead to them becoming prom knights.
