Once upon a time I was in love with a wonderful, amazing man. Through a terrible loss I suffered with coping with that loss, and wondering if it would ever be possible for me to love someone else the way I loved him again. It's been nearly 8 years, and although some days I still struggle with the loss of losing someone I loved so very much, with the love and support of my family, friends, and my faith I am once able to believe that through loss there can be love again. This book is a detailing of all the wonderful things I am thinking while waiting, as well as all the things I anticipate being able to say about finding my happily ever after once he comes.
