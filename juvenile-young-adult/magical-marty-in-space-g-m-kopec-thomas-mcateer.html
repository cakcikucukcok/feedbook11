Magical Marty in Space is the first story of the Magical Marty Reading Adventure Series, which takes you on a journey into the cosmos with two cool friends, Magical Marty and Jimmy. Magical Marty can surely touch the stars! Blast off into space and discover the constellations; Orion, the Big Dipper and Leo the Lion, a wiggly snake, a flying giraffe and so much more! Join Magical Marty and explore space in a fun and wacky way in this entertaining picture book.

The Magical Marty Reading Adventure Series is a great way to engage children to read again. Reading to your children fifteen minutes a day is a proven activity that encourages them to become life long readers! Preschool and early elementary children will enjoy these wacky adventures with humor thrown in and still remember the focused content. They will also learn about the important aspects of friendship and imagination.
