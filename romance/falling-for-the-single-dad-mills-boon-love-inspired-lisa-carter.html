Finding Her Way Home

After fifteen years away, Dr. Caroline Duer is nervous about returning to her hometown. He won’t go through the same pain again. But Izzie isn’t the only one tumbling head over heels for the enigmatic Caroline. And if she can release the pain of the past, she just might be the missing piece he and his daughter have been searching for.
