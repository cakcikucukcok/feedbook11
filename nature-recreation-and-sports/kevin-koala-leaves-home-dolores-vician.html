This book is about a curious young koala bear named Kevin. Kevin is tired of spending his days in a eucalyptus tree with his mother and other koala bears. He wonders about life beyond this tree. Therefore, one day he decides to climb down from the tree and go exploring. ... He soon discovers the animals that live nearby: a wombat, a kangaroo, an emu and a Tasmanian devil. He sees how they look, how they live and what they eat. He soon decides that life in a eucalyptus tree with his family is a good one for him.
