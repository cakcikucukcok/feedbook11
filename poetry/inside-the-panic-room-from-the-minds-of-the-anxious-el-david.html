This book will be a beacon of hope for those who suffer from anxiety, to know they are not alone, and that anxiety is not a "STOP" Sign. This anthology will send a clear message to the non-anxious, that the anxious are normal people that go through this turbulent reality, warping through time to see the worst, in order to create the best. This anthology is not only a great compilation of poetry that witll stand the test of time, but will also serve as a cornerstone for poets and artists that deal with anxiety to build on, fear forward, and take stages that beckon.
