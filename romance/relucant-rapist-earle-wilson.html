But that was gone that was all in the past. The mob had set me up I cannot understand by how could I have been so stupid the mob planned the setup and I was fooled but then so was the whole department fell for the scam. I was charged with rape and I was fired but there had been no rape I was innocent. But just the fact that I had been charged was all the commission needed. They did not actually fire me they had no cause. I was a burr under their ass before I was in narcotics; I was in robbery and murder when there was a drug dealer killed it was natural for me to follow up on the murder and I complained like hell about not getting any support.
