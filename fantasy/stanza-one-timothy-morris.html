The legend of the Stormhammer is one of Vosteros' oldest tales. It goes back to before the first Cataclysm and stretches past the Collision of Worlds. It is a legend that started a long time ago amongst the Subarii; Vosteros' most proud clan of warriors. As time passed, the legend, as most legends do, slipped into fable and folklore, a quaint story told to children to keep them on the right path. It isn't until after the Collision that all of Vosteros is reminded that all legends originate in truth. The Ballad of Bayan Stormhammer is the story of the legend's focal piece, the one who would grow to simply be known as "The Stormhammer." A child born in war with a mission of enforcing peace.
