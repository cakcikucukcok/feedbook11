Do you ever wonder why you click with certain friends and family members? Often it is because you like to do the same things. But what if your friends or family members did something you werent comfortable with? What would you do?

Thats the dilemma faced by a hyena in author Jennifer Lohrfink Morfeas The Little Hyena. Because he does not want to laugh at others, like the members of his pack do, they turn on him. Now, those he thought were friends bully him and dont want to play with him anymore.

The Little Hyena tells how he remains true to himself while deflecting the bullying. Learn how he makes the situation best for himself, those being bullied, and the greater good. Told in rhyme and with illustrations by Erik Draw, the story is entertaining while teaching a valuable lesson.

Look for more of Little Hyenas friends in upcoming books!
