EQ Applied: The Real-World Guide to Emotional Intelligence by Justin Bariso | Conversation Starters

EQ Applied: The Real-World Guide to Emotional Intelligence is the debut book of author Justin Bariso. The whole world now lives in the age of social media. People are plagued with constant distraction. With this come social media attacks, rampant corruption, among others. A high EQ or emotional intelligence quotience is needed more than ever. Justin Bariso brings the age-old concept of emotional intelligence into the modern real world. He combines volumes of scientific research, personal stories and high-profile examples to bring us his discoveries in EQ Applied. 

What Got You Here Won’t Get You There author Marshall Goldsmith says that EQ Applied is “a joy to read… a great asset to companies.” Emmy Award–winning journalist Rebeccaa Jarvis says that Justin Bariso’s must-read book “shows us a different side to emotional intelligence—and then teaches readers how to grow their own, step by step.”
