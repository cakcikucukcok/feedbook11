Expressive and colorful, More Coffee, Please! allures and captivates readers. Its language is both amatory and sensuous. Racy, it entices one with vivid verse. Seductive, it evokes desire.

More Coffee, Please! combines old-world methods with new-wave fashion. While its voice and style are contemporary, its rhyming verse is reminiscent of old-fashioned form. The outcome is both traditional and modern.

More Coffee, Please! is a must read for coffee-lovers. Essentially, the love poetry revolves around coffee and fantasy. Among others, some of the coffee flavors include Irish cream, hazelnut, French roast and vanilla cream.

Fictitious, the book describes amorous relationships. Sexy, it portrays exotic situations. Lovable, it enchants with light topics. Overall, More Coffee, Please! is a book to enjoy.
