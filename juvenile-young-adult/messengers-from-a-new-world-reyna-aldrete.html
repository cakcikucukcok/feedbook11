Maria de los Angeles searches for true love. In that process, she discovers herself through communication with a new world, with nature and with the messengers. Her childhood environment, shaped by the precious company of her grandmother, becomes a new world that is always present and available to everyone, if we take the time to pay attention and not lose sight.

Discovering the magic of love gives wisdom and understanding to the daily life of the girl. Each time she releases one of her stifling fears, there is a wonderful connection that makes her more conscious and sure of whom she really is.
