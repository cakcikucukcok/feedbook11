About the Author:

Montana Madness is Sioux Dallas' seventh book, and she has two more currently in progress. Dallas selects a true event or real people and builds a story around it.

Dallas has written short stories since she was in the third grade and heard a great uncle, who became famous, tell his original stories. By the seventh grade, teachers were encouraging her to do something with her work.

Teaching school, keeping house and family, raising and training horses, giving riding lessons, music, church work and community endeavors all kept her too busy. After Dallas retired and became a widow she took steps to have her work published. She expresses gratitude constantly for the love and support of family and friends.
