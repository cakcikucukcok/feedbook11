It was close to (15-years) ago, since the LORD healed me (MIRACULOUSLY). I was once in the hospital giving birth to my daughter, then I was In a (COMA-for 2-months) I thank GOD for my mother, a STRONG woman of GOD, she kept her FAITH As she stayed by my Hospital-Bed day after day, Trusting and Believing that the LORD would Heal me, as he DID. In the (year of 1991) my LORD and SAVIOR gave me A second chance at LIFE. God then allowed me a new start, Blessing me with the privilege to once again use my MIND and HEART. He also allowed me the HONOR to PRAISE and WORSHIP his Holy name, as I began to live a RIGHTEOUS Life glorifying our Lord and Savior JESUS CHRIST. I thank and praise the Lord continuously, but most of all strive To worship him Faithfully for allowing his child to be. As I share the words that he instill within me, so that you will have The opportunity to read GODs POETRY and receive them JOYFULLY. As I read and receive God Holy Word, I am blessed with the chance To feed my Heart, Mind and Soul. If you read these scriptures St. LUKE 8: 40-56, you may be able to understand how grateful I am to my HEAVENLY FATHER. As he allow me to nap for a little while then he woke me Back -Up and Said (COME). And I was Obedient and (ROSE) again to do his (WILL) In my life and to be the mother for my daughters. AMEN.
