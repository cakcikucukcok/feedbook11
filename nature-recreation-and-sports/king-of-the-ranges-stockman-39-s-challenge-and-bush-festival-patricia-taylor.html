King of the Ranges Stockmans Challenge and Bush Festival Murrurundi Celebrating 15 Years To help celebrate the fifteenth anniversary of the King of the Ranges Stockmans Challenge and Bush Festival in Murrurundi, this book offers ninety-nine photographs taken by various members of the committee and photographers that have attended the event since its inaugural event in 2004. Competitors emulate the skills of the early Australian stockman Archie Bung McInnes, who was known as the King of the Ranges around the Upper Hunter district. The photos selected have captured the action and excitement of each annual event as competitors vie for the title of King of the Ranges. This book is a piece of local history and a wonderful keepsake.
