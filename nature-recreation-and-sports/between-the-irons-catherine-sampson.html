Catherine Sampsons devotion to horses and her quest to understand them on a personal level are revealed in this story of an equestrians metamorphosis.

On the journey from naive beginner to master trainer, an awakening occurs that culminates in a life-altering spiritual bond and a remarkable career.

Between the Irons is a story that spans the continents, from the northern timberlands in Canada to the south of Spain, and on to the wilds of Africa. Follow Catherines trail of euphoria, fear, tragedy, and resolution: a trail that continues today as she educates and inspires future riders.
