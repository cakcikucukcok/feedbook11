Spirit Child, a book for all faiths, encourages children to embrace their early-life, spiritual connections to God as true and to be trusted.

Children, today, are coming to us a bit differently than previous generations.  They operate with fewer filters and with a new kind of intelligence that affects how they learn and process.

What one could call gifts of the spirit: imaginary friends, strong intuition, spiritual senses (clairaudience, clairvoyance, clairsentience, claircognizance) visitations from loved ones, angels, and guides, astral travel, and a deep connection to nature can easily shut down if a child’s spiritual reality is not recognized and nurtured.

The world needs these children, who experience life beyond the physical, to move us forward to a new way of being on Earth.  Spirit Child provides an opportunity to support each child on their unique path as they navigate a world where their gifts often make them appear sensitive or feel isolated.

Vibrant and healing images, offered by energy artist, Jane Delaford Taylor, perfectly bring the intention of the words on each page to life.

Spirit Child is intended to open conversations between child and parent that might otherwise go unrealized.  Created for both young and old, this is a book adults will enjoy as much as children.
