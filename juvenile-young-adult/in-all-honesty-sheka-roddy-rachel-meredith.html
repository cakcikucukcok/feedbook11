In All Honesty is a story of a loving family of four. Cindy, the oldest daughter, is very adventurous and Brandy, her little sister, just thinks Cindy is soo cool! You will follow Cindy as she pretends and goes on an adventure and creates a situation in which she is reminded of the importance of telling the truth. This story highlights the importance of being honest. In All Honesty highlights the biggest point that even in times when being dishonest may seem like the easy way out, being honest is at all times the best way out. Being honest in all situations or jams in life bring forth many rewards. The freeness of embracing honesty leaves us free to safeguard ourselves from guilt and unnecessary burden. This story shares that all things have consequences. Even the smallest things in life can create a great teaching moment for our little ones and even one for us young at heart to reflect on. In this case, even a glass of water can be utilized to teach a valuable lesson in life.
