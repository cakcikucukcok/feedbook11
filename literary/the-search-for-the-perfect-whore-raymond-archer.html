I think we were both looking for the perfect whore, though our views about that differed. Perhaps in our search for the perfect whore we would find the Virgin Mother herself.

Archer's carousing leads him into sometimes exciting and sometimes dangerous adventures. But will he be successful in The Search for the Perfect Whore?
