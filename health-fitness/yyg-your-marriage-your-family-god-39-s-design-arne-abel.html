"YYG | Your Marriage. Your Family. God's Design" subtitled "Making Sense of Becoming One Flesh" is for couples who want to equip and strengthen their relationship in the foundational principles of The Marriage Covenant God established at Creation. If you want to understand and live God's Design of your relationship, then you too can Become One Flesh.

The focus is on:

  • why Becoming One Flesh is key to a godly relationship; what it looks like and how you get there
  • how God crafted the man and woman; and why His Design is so important to Becoming One Flesh
  • the Biblical meaning of Love, Forgiveness, and Repentance; and how you can unBecome One Flesh if you're not well-equipped
  • believing God crafted man as Family Shepherd; and how your sons grow from Family Shepherds-to-be to claim the mantle of Family Shepherd

Believing the cause of family breakdown is found in the pandemic of fatherless homes and the devastating effect of No Fault Divorce, then perhaps the time has come for a Marriage Revival. With "YYG", let's go back to "In the Beginning" to restore God's Design of His Marriage Covenant and bring Becoming One Flesh back to life!
