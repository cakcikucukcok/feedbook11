Rugged and strong, Chance McCall was an awakening, a mystery, a dream -- and Jennifer Ann Tyler loved him from the moment he hired on at the Triple T Ranch. Now a tragic accident has robbed Chance of his memory -- and he must leave the ranch to retrieve his lost past.
