Have you been taken advantage of in an intimate relationship? Has someone used your affection for their selfi sh gain? Do you know someone who has been taken advantage of because of their affection for another? If you have experienced a situation like this, After the Relationship Con is a book that was written for you. Although con situations have been written about, there is minimal information for those who have suffered the affects of being conned within a love relationship. And no one has addressed the serious emotional issues faced by the victims of relationship cons until now. After the Relationship Con provides compelling information from a therapist on how to recognize and heal from the destructive affects of having been conned in a relationship. Finally, there is recognition that everyone doesnt just bounce back when a relationship ends. And when someone feels as though they have been taken advantage of, there is a deeper level to their pain. Sometimes we need help moving forward. After the Relationship Con; How do I Pick up and Move On? provides your answer.
