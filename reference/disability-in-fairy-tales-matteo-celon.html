After being operated on for cancer in 2011, now seeks new studies in the field of nursing. Remains fascinated by this world, and composed his first opera "there is no place for us". Also performs voluntary work at the civil protection of Agna (PD) and Ready, where volunteer Conselve plays welfare type/rescuer. At this time he devoted himself to the writing of his biography as well as the writing of her first novel, titled "The red dress".
