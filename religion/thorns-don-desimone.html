You deserve to live a life full of love, happiness, and peace.

You deserve to feel the contentment that comes from living out God’s purpose for you.

You deserve to maximize and achieve your God-given potential.

However, it’s impossible to do all that with a soul full of fear, a soul full of Thorns.

Learn how to recognize, confront, and remove your deepest fears—some of which you may not even be aware of.

As you remove deeply held fears of such things as abandonment, betrayal, judgment, rejection, and failure—or even of good things like God, love, and success—you will make more room inside yourself for God. This will allow you to

• live a happier, more joyful life;

• find peace for yourself;

• bring more love into your life;

• find and live your purpose; and

• maximize and realize your true potential.
