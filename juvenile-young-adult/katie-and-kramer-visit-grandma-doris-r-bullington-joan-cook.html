Katie and Kramer make a visit to the country in the fall of the year to see Grandma Margarets grandchildren and animals who live around the home. By learning the different colors, the ABCs and names of the animals, Doris hopes to help children learn about loving grandma, grandpa, and the many animals in America.

Katie and Kramer play in the leaves, chase butterfl ies, and search for birds, ducks,and other animals. They discover Papas beautiful horses, deer, and many other animals found on the small country farm. Grandma loved to have her grandchildren visit and look for the beautiful animals whom she cares for everyday.

This is Katie and Kramers third book of their adventures as adopted doggies. They act like children as one is shy and the other busy. I hope you enjoy reading this amazing story of the doggies, the children, Grandpa and Grandma, and the beautiful animals.
