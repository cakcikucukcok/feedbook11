Have you ever wondered if the Apostle Judas Iscariot went to heaven or hell? Without Judas, could there have been the body and blood sacrifice and the resurrection that, as all Judeo-Christians believe, was the ultimate price for our salvation? Did God destine Judas, even before his birth, to be mankinds representative at this sacrificial altar that caused the oblation of The Lamb of God?

Judas: The Man From Kerioth examines this possibility and draws its own conclusion from established biblical information.

It is a long overdue life story of the most misunderstood Apostle, chosen by God to help fulfill biblical prophecy. While some of Judas Iscariots life is developed fictionally in this novel for continuity, it closely follows all that is presently known about him today.

This book is not intended to change anyones mind or their own beliefs. It is only intended to examine other possibilities that can be supported by biblical writings just as well as any other conclusion.
